About Us

We are two life long friends, who share a passion for antiquities and estate sale planning.

Who We Are

Our names are Desire√© and Pam. We met each other as young adults working together at a nursing home while attending college in Billings, Montana. Although we have moved away, now living and operating out of Arizona, our values still stem from our upbringing up north, where hard work, family, and the community are all very important to us. We fell in love with antiquities, and the stories that they can tell us. This eventually led us to estate sales which we hold great passion for.

Our Vision

When going to estate sales, we always love to get to know the owners of the home. We noticed that a lot of the time, they mention how tedious and difficult the process was to start, manage, and finish an estate sale. Estate sales are often needed for a loss in the family, and the pieces they sell are usually priceless treasures that hold sentimental value to the owner of the home. Because of this, it was sad to hear their struggle. We made it our mission to change this, creating A Lifetime of Treasures, seeking to offer everyone a pleasant and seamless experience when the time to start an estate sale comes around.

Our Values

At A Lifetime of Treasures, we value family and the community. We also believe in hard work, attention to detail, a warm personality, and taking pride in what we do.
