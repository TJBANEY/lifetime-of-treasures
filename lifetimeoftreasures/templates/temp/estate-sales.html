Estate Sale Planning

Thinking about setting up an estate sale, but not sure where to start? Contact us and we can take that stress off your back: planning, organizing, and managing the entire process.

What Estate Sale Planning Includes

Cataloging

Going through your home and cataloging the things that you wish to get rid of at an estate sale, can be tolling, tedious, and consume much of your time. We will catalog all your assets that you wish to part with so you can sit back and relax.

Inventory Appraisal

At A Lifetime of Treasures, we believe we have seen it all: jewelery, furniture, art, clothing, and much more. If you're not sure what the value of one of your items is, let us assist you. It could be more valuable than you think!

Advertisement

While setting up your estate sale, we will also advertise the event for you. We use multiple avenues of advertisement such as social media, newspapers, and many other online web sites to maximize the exposure you deserve. We are flexible as well and could use any other platform for advertisement you would like.

Transactions

Sold! One of your pieces has been purchased, and you need to be paid of course. We will setup a point of sale area to maximize your earnings. This includes card readers for secure payment processing from debit and credit cards to your bank. We also have a very keen eye for paper cash, even collecting different kinds as a hobby, so you can rest assured knowing counterfeits will be spotted.

Visitor Assistance

Warm personalities, strong soft skills and customer service are core values we have here at A Lifetime of Treasures. During the day of your estate sale, we will make our way around the home, helping potential buyers with any questions they have, while making them feel comfortable to browse your inventory.

Unsold Inventory

If you have any unsold property after the duration of your estate sale, we can help you sell it through other third party services and applications.
