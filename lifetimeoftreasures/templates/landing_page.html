Estate Sale Planning

Sometimes in life we have to let go of some of our most prized possessions. We know this can be difficult on top of the tedious process of setting up an estate sale. At A Lifetime of Treasures, we take that stress off your back, planning, organizing, and managing the entire process. From cataloging all of the items that you are parting ways with, to advertising the sale, all the way to managing the sale while it's happening, we take care of it all.

Who We Are

We are two friends from Billings Montana who have known each other since we were young adults, and share the same passion for antiquities and estate sales. Together, we started A Lifetime of Treasures, because we cherish family values and understand the importance of priceless treasures such as heirlooms, and understand how difficult it is to part with them.
